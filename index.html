  • Vicious Walker

    The vicious walker is a simple model for chemical reactions as these processes require molecules to meet and react. This model contains walker in an n dimensional space on a grid and they move every time step in a random direction. When they meet they annihilate. In this project I have studied the time frame of these reactions as well as what I have called a friendly walker.
